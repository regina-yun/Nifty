What kind of products can I add to this list?

Major cosmetic products, perfumes, shampoos, body washes, lotions, etc.

How to add and delete items?

Identify if the product contains an expiry date.

If yes, kindly enter the full expiry date and leave 'Period-After-Opening'(PAO) entry as '--'.

If no, kindly enter the date of opening and select the PAO value stated on the label.

Click on ‘Click here to submit’ and the name and expiry date will be calculated and printed accordingly.

Click on to remove an entry from the list.

Expiration dates in cosmetics.

Most cosmetics' expiry date is based on when it was first opened. How long the product can be used depends on the 'Period-After-Opening' (PAO). It is a symbol with a numerical value on it. The numerical value refers to the shelf life in months. E.g., if the PAO states “12”, it will expire 12 months after opening.
