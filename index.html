How to add and delete items?

Identify if the product contains an expiry date.

If yes, kindly enter the full expiry date.

Expiration dates in cosmetics.

Most cosmetics' expiry date is based on when it was first opened.
How long the product lasts depends on the 'Period-After-Opening' (PAO). It is a symbol with a numerical value on it.



The numerical value refers to the shelf life in months.
E.g., if the PAO states “12”, it will expire 12 months after opening.
